Gab-i Sang Maragtas


Date: September 5, 2023

𝐆𝐞𝐭 𝐫𝐞𝐚𝐝𝐲 𝐭𝐨 𝘄𝗶𝘁𝗻𝗲𝘀𝘀 𝐭𝐡𝐞 𝐫𝐢𝐜𝐡 𝐜𝐮𝐥𝐭𝐮𝐫𝐞 𝐚𝐧𝐝 𝐡𝐞𝐫𝐢𝐭𝐚𝐠𝐞 𝐨𝐟 𝘁𝗵𝗲 𝗣𝗿𝗼𝘃𝗶𝗻𝗰𝗲 𝗼𝗳 𝐂𝐚𝐩𝐢𝐳 𝐚𝐭 𝐭𝐡𝐞 𝐆𝐚𝐛-𝐢 𝐬𝐚𝐧𝐠 𝐌𝐚𝐫𝐚𝐠𝐭𝐚𝐬 𝐂𝐮𝐥𝐭𝐮𝐫𝐚𝐥 𝐒𝐡𝐨𝐰 𝗵𝗲𝗹𝗱 𝗶𝗻 𝗰𝗲𝗹𝗲𝗯𝗿𝗮𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻 𝗼𝗳 𝘁𝗵𝗲 𝐁𝐮𝐲𝐥𝐨𝐠𝐚𝐧𝐚𝐲 𝐅𝐞𝐬𝐭𝐢𝐯𝐚𝐥 𝟐𝟎𝟐𝟑! Join us on this meaningful journey through history as we present Gab-i sang Maragtas Cultural Show presenting "Dali sa Capiz" - a play portraying the beauty of the province, and the resilience and triumphs of the Capisnon people, as also reflected in the Buyloganay culture of the Ivisanons. The name of the festival was drawn from the Ivisanons' spirit of Buyloganay and way of life characterized by the gesture of unity and cooperation among the town's residents. Buyloganay is also greatly manifested when the people of Ivisan choose to rise and celebrate life with optimism and resilience amid challenging situations. This event is made possible through the collaborative efforts of the Provincial Government of Capiz, led by Governor Fredenil H. Castro, the Provincial Tourism and Cultural Affairs Office, the Municipal Government of Ivisan, and its Municipal Tourism Office. Performing for Gab-i sang Maragtas are the following: ▪️Niña Jaro, Tawag ng Tanghalan Season 6 Quarterfinalist ▪️Peter John Acebuque, Grand Winner, Bugal sang Capiz: Search for Capiz Singing Ambassador 2020 ▪️Clarisse Anne Alvarez ▪️Arnel Heremias, Capisnon local artist and multi-intrumentalist ▪️Colegio de la Purisima Concepcion Chorale ▪️Filamer Christian University Silak Dance Company ▪️Dagway Sigmahanon, Inc., Galing Pook Awardee for Trailblazing Program ▪️Panay Bukidnon Community of Tapaz, Capiz ▪️Municipality of Ivisan Maragtas sang Capisnon Performers ▪️Municipality of Ivisan Saot Capiz 2023 Folk Dancers Let's come together to celebrate the spirit of Buyloganay that resonate not only from the Ivisanons but all Capisnons! See you at the Freedom Plaza of the Municipality of Ivisan this September 7, 2023 at 7:00 PM! #BuyloganayFestival2023 #CaptureCapiz #PTCAOat2023 #WowOneCapiz #CaptureFestivities
