Join us on this meaningful journey through history as we present Gab-i sang Maragtas Cultural Show presenting "Dali sa Capiz" - a play portraying the beauty of the province, and the resilience and triumphs of the Capisnon people, as also reflected in the Buyloganay culture of the Ivisanons. The name of the festival was drawn from the Ivisanons' spirit of Buyloganay and way of life characterized by the gesture of unity and cooperation among the town's residents. Buyloganay is also greatly manifested when the people of Ivisan choose to rise and celebrate life with optimism and resilience amid challenging situations. This event is made possible through the collaborative efforts of the Provincial Government of Capiz, led by Governor Fredenil H. Castro, the Provincial Tourism and Cultural Affairs Office, the Municipal Government of Ivisan, and its Municipal Tourism Office.
